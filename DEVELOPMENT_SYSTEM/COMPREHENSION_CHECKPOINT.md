# Comprehension Checkpoint

Before proposing edits, answer from the package:
1. What exact version/build is the baseline?
2. Which documents are authoritative, and what resolves a contradiction?
3. Name five immutable principles.
4. What is implemented but still requires live deployment verification?
5. What are the highest-priority resurfaced backlog items?
6. What failure patterns must not recur?
7. What may be challenged, and what requires explicit constitutional approval?
8. What is the required response to an unexpected discrepancy?
9. What evidence is required before saying a UI or synchronization path works?
10. What fresh perspective would you investigate first, without editing code?

Failure or uncertainty requires more reading, not guessing.
