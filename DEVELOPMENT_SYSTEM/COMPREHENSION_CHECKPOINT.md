6. What is implemented but still requires deployed, credentialed, or physical-device verification?
7. What are the highest-priority resurfaced backlog items?
8. Which failure patterns from ERR-023 through ERR-026 must not recur?
9. What is the required response to an unexpected discrepancy or an external-review claim lacking reproduction?
10. What evidence is required before saying responsive UI, context preservation, synchronization, or correction propagation works?
11. How must ambiguous legacy activity categories be migrated?
12. What fresh perspective would you investigate first without editing code?
