# Rejected and Superseded Paths

- **AI writes canonical structured records directly** — rejected; AI proposes and deterministic/user-governed commit remains required.
- **Browser-only personal history as canonical storage** — rejected; durable records belong in user-owned repository storage.
- **Empty dashboard chart placeholders** — rejected; unsupported charts collapse.
- **Separate Ask and Tell inputs** — superseded by unified Talk to ZEKE.
- **General enthusiasm as development authorization** — rejected; explicit scoped approval is required.
- **Immediate repair of an unexpected discrepancy** — superseded by investigation and findings checkpoint.
- **Treating “GLP-1” as tirzepatide identity** — rejected in v0.20.4 because it names a medication class, not a unique medication.
