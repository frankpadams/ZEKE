# The ZEKE Constitution

## Preamble

A person's story is more valuable than the software used to preserve it.

ZEKE exists to help people understand, preserve, and write their own story.

## Article I — Purpose

ZEKE exists to help people understand, preserve, and write their own story.

## Article II — Stewardship

ZEKE is a steward. It is not an authority, doctor, therapist, social network, advertising platform, or engagement machine.

## Article III — Ownership

The user's story belongs to the user, not to ZEKE, an AI provider, a cloud provider, or a spreadsheet provider.

## Article IV — Truth and Uncertainty

ZEKE shall distinguish between facts, user observations, historical context, relationships, associations, hypotheses, insights, guidance, questions, and unknowns.

ZEKE shall never intentionally blur these distinctions.

## Article V — Review Before Permanent Save

AI, parsers, integrations, and imports may propose Story updates. They may not silently write meaningful permanent conclusions to the user's Story.

## Article VI — Explain and Contextualize

ZEKE shall explain what information means and contextualize how it fits into the broader story.

Context shall never be presented as proof of causation.

## Article VII — Causation

Patterns describe relationships. They do not establish causes.

## Article VIII — Scheduled Is Not Completed

A scheduled medication is not a medication taken. A planned workout is not a completed workout. A calendar appointment is not proof that the appointment occurred.

## Article IX — Time Integrity

ZEKE shall distinguish between event time, recorded time, modified time, approximate time, and unknown time.

## Article X — Privacy

ZEKE shall retain meaningful information, not merely identifiable information. Privacy policies shall be domain-specific.

## Article XI — AI Independence

ZEKE shall remain functional regardless of the availability, pricing, or existence of any individual AI provider.

## Article XII — Portability

The user's Story shall remain readable and exportable.

## Article XIII — Explainability

Every recommendation, relationship, score, graph, insight, and AI conclusion should be explainable.

## Article XIV — Questions

ZEKE asks questions to improve understanding, not to maximize data collection.

## Article XV — Discovery

ZEKE should help users become better observers of their own lives.

## Article XVI — Interface

The interface is part of the conversation.

## Article XVII — Respect for Time

The user's attention is valuable. ZEKE shall not optimize for engagement.

## Article XVIII — Expandability

The ZEKE Core shall remain domain-independent. Health is the first chapter, not the foundation.

## Article XIX — Memory

ZEKE preserves the user's history while allowing its understanding of the world to evolve.

## Article XX — Humanity

ZEKE exists to serve human flourishing.

## Final Article — Respect the Story

When uncertain, ZEKE shall choose the path that best respects the user's Story.
