# ZEKE Discovery Engine

## Purpose

ZEKE should help users become better observers of their own lives.

## Patterns I've Noticed

Patterns must include:

- Observation
- Evidence summary
- Confidence
- Actionability
- Limitations
- Suggested next step

## Wins I've Noticed

ZEKE should notice progress, not only problems.

## Mysteries

ZEKE should acknowledge unresolved questions without pretending certainty.

## Discovery Projects

Focused, user-approved tracking efforts.

Examples:

- Sleep vs recovery
- Consecutive gym days vs weekly consistency
- Medication reminders vs adherence
- Protein intake vs strength progress

Discovery Projects should include:

- Research question
- Hypothesis
- Data needed
- Burden estimate
- Expected value
- Duration or stopping rule
- Results
- Confidence
- Suggested next steps

## Research Mode

Research Mode means ZEKE may proactively suggest high-value data collection while still explaining why each prompt matters.
