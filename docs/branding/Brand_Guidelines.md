# Project ZEKE Brand Reference

## Core idea
**One thread. Everything connected.** The folded blue ribbon Z represents a continuous thread weaving through health, home, pets, documents, vehicles, and future life-management modules.

## Palette
- Deep navy: `#030C1C`
- Midnight blue: `#1E3A5F`
- Intelligence blue: `#2D6A88`
- Teal/cyan accent: `#4DB6AC`
- Restrained gold: `#D4AF37`

Gold is reserved for high-value emphasis: important insights, priority questions, milestones, and key brand moments. It should not dominate routine controls.

## Product use
- The ribbon Z may appear in navigation, splash/loading states, favicon/app icons, and the Talk to ZEKE affordance.
- The tagline belongs in onboarding, About, empty states, and occasional brand moments—not on every working screen.
- Product UI remains data-dense, calm, and readable. Branding supports the experience rather than competing with it.
